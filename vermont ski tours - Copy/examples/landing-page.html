Dear Skiers and Snowboarders,

"Welcome to Vermont." This year I am celebrating my 52nd season sending over 200,000 skiers and snowboarders to Vermont. Whether you take one of our many ski buses or drive yourself, you can leave your cares behind because we will be waiting there with open arms to welcome you on your quest. In partnership with Vermont ski areas and hotels, we have designed our trips with you in mind. This season we will be offering multiple programs to 6 major ski areas in Vermont: Mount Snow, Stratton, Okemo, Killington, Sugarbush and Stowe. Once you have looked through the Vermont Ski Tours 2019 Individual and Group Ski Travel booklet, we hope you will be as excited about the upcoming ski season as we are.

So what is winter in the Green Moutain State all about

Vermont is home to some of the best skiing and snowboard the East coast has to offer. Weather you like the long cruising green trails or the thrilling double diamonds or if you would perfer to sit back and enjoy apres ski, Vermont has something to offer for everyone.

For over 50 years now Vermont Ski tours has specialized in bringing people from all over to their favorite moutains. We are commited to fighting the high cost of skiing and snowboarding, while at the same time meeting the varying demands of our customers.

We make it easy for you to enjoy a successuful ski trip via bus. A single phone call will confirm bus transportation, lodging, meals, and lift tickets. We will provide group leaders with our Group Organizer's Trip Kit which includes everything you need for a successful ski weekend. We offer a wide variety of custom trips and remeber: "NOBODY BEATS OUR PRICES"
